Summer 2023 Work Term Report

Introduction

Welcome to my second work term report! In this report, I will go over the second 4-month term out of my two 8 month co-op term (Sept to Dec 2023) at Nissan Canada Inc, what I accomplished, the skills I developed, key takeaways.

About Nissan Canada

Established in 1933, Nissan is renowned multinational automotive manufacturer and stands as a global leader in the automotive industry, known for its innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainability. Nissan Canada Inc. is headquartered in Mississauga, ON, Canada with over 400 employees while the main Nissan Motor Corporation is headquartered in Yokohama, Japan. Nissan's models cater to diverse markets with an extensive range of vehicles, including electric cars, SUVs, trucks, and commercial vehicles. INFINITI is Nissan's luxury vehicle division known for sophisticated design and comfort.

Vehicle Connected Services is a rapidly growing field within the automotive industry that leverages computer science and technology to provide innovative and enhanced experiences for vehicle owners. Since modern vehicles are equipped with sophisticated infotainment and connectivity systems, creating user-friendly interfaces, integrating smartphone connectivity, and ensuring seamless communication between vehicles and external systems is crucial.

Joining the team at Nissan has been a remarkable chapter in my co-op journey. As a global leader in the automotive industry, Nissan's reputation for innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainability with their lineup of EV's has been evident from day one.

Job Description

As a Vehicle Connected Services Co-op at Nissan Canada Inc., my role was centered around actively participating in the development and testing of mobile apps and web portals. Working closely with a dedicated team, I had the exciting opportunity to contribute to the creation of innovative solutions that enhance the connectivity and experience of Nissan and INFINITI vehicle owners. My top responsibilities included collaborating on app features, engaging in testing procedures to ensure app functionality and improving current VCS processes using automation.

One of the unique aspects of my job was the opportunity to be hands-on with the vehicles themselves and directly engage in testing the functionality of in-vehicle features and mobile apps. This experience allowed me to bridge the gap between software development and real-world automotive technology. My SQL skills were important, as it enabled me to interact with databases, extract valuable insights, and ensure efficient data management. Proficiency in utilizing JIRA was essential for reporting issues, tracking progress of bug fixes, and validating them. Collaboration was also key, as I actively participated in cross-functional teams, collaborating with developers, designers, and marketing teams to achieve our shared objectives. My ability to contribute effectively in team settings and communicate technical concepts clearly was pivotal in driving project success. Although I have learned these skills in class and from previous experiences, this role enabled me to leverage these for real-world applications. This combination of these skills was important in not only fulfilling the requirements of the role but also in (literally) driving innovation for Vehicle Connected Services at Nissan Canada.



Learning Goals & Reflections

I am delighted to reflect on successfully acheving the learning goals I had set for this term. My goals were closed tied to my job tasks, and while some aspects of these objectives fell beyond my immediate job scope, I was determined to go above and beyond through collaborative efforts with cross-functional teams and use of technological tools, I have expanded my skill set and accomplished these goals. The knowledge and expertise I have gained, particularly in app development, vehicle connected technology, and automotive business operations, have not only enhanced my current role but will also certainly prove invaluable in my future automotive work experiences. I led the EV Day presentation at the Vehicle Connected Services booth, collaborating alongside my coordinator Roma. All the positive feedback from colleagues, managers, and event attendees acknowledging the success of the booth and communicating the information, was incredibly rewarding. Additionally, I actively participated in volunteer opportunities, showcasing vehicles for the training team in collaboration with the Marketing Department. These experiences allowed me to demonstrate and further refine my oral communication skills, creating a clearer and more engaging interaction within cross-functional teams and events.

Despite an increase in workload, I maintained consistent standards by completing assignments with the same level of quality. Utilizing Tableau and engaging in discussions with the IS team allowed me to make informed decisions and provide recommendations based on the outcomes of my assignments. Rather than solely delivering expected outcomes, I actively contributed to making recommendations and strategies, emphasizing the end goal and potential actions to be taken. Additionally, despite the increase in vehicle sales volume for particular models, I successfully managed and executed a larger quantity of in-vehicle messages, demonstrating improved proficiency in managing quantitative tasks amidst increased work demands.


Enhancing my technological literacy has significantly elevated the quality of my work, through accuracy, thoroughness, and timeliness. Implementing advanced Excel operations in the VCS Availability Sheet using automation code, IF-ELSE statements, etc. enabled a notable improvement in task completion. Specifically, tasks that were previously expected by end-of-day were now consistently accomplished earlier, reflecting increased efficiency and time management. This elevation in technological literacy not only improved the quality of deliverables but also allowed for a more proactive approach to completing assignments, positively impacting overall workflow and productivity.

Achieving proficiency in personal organization and time management has been important in refining my workflow. By allotting in-person tasks for the designated office days, I ensure maximum productivity during those moments. Especially since my role involves working directly with the vehicles to perform various testing and diagnostics during in-person days, I collaborate with my team members during those days to ensure effective and timely task completion. Moreover, I have written and updated the documentation for the upcoming intern through a structured and organized process, ensuring a smooth transition while demonstrating effective communication. Additionally, coordinating and organizing all aspects of EV Day planning alongside my manager showcased improved time management skills, ensuring a well-executed event while balancing other responsibilities. This has demonstrated my ability to balance and structure task management which has significantly improved overall productivity and quality.


Conclusion

In conclusion, my first co-op work term at Nissan Canada has been a fulfilling and enlightening experience thus far. I am grateful I had the opportunity to learn many new technologies and strengthen my skills with those I was already familiar with. Although some of the tools and frameworks I have used was new to me, I ramped myself up and continued to push the scope of my work. If asked to describe the contents of my first co-op work term, I see my audience emphasizing the hands-on experience gained in mobile app development process and testing procedures, the practical insights into vehicle connected services, and effectively collaborating with cross-functional teams to acheive my learning outcomes. With four months of valuable experience behind me and an exciting four-month journey still ahead, I am excited to expand my knowledge and broaden my skill set during this co-op, and I am enthusiastic about the positive impact these experiences will have on this second term as well as my future career within the automotive industry.
